Banyan Tree School

School will provide, in partnership with the parents, a stimulating learning environment in which every child is cared for and valued for their individuality, creativity and heritage. Within a green, safe and caring campus we will ensure that students of all ability levels are able to excel.

School will encourage each student to achieve his or her full potential by promoting curiosity and enthusiasm and equip them for the demands and opportunities of the future.
We will make every effort to ensure that our children leave school with:
1. A set of spiritual and moral values – honesty, integrity and good judgement.
2. An enquiring and discriminating mind and a desire for knowledge.
3. Strong self-esteem and high personal expectation.
4. Independence and confidence.
5. Tolerance and respect for others.
School believes that every child is entitled to enjoy his/her childhood. We want our children to take with them happy memories of the years spent with us.
