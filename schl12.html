RIMTL World School

RIMT WORLD SCHOOL, ideally situated opposite Green and Serene Shivalik Gardens at Manimajra, Chandigarh, is the Educational venture of well known and reputed RIMT University and Schools and Institutes. The school is the dream project of the Founder Chairman Dr. Hukam Chand Bansal, a visionary educationist and industrialist who always nurtured the dream of building a utopian knowledge centre that will nourish the mind, body and soul of every child, thus shaping the individual into a versatile global citizen.

School's Mission:

To ‘Nurture Excellence’ , by delivering a student centric curriculum in a very conducive, caring and stimulating environment where the learners realize their potential and grow into responsible citizens and global leaders. ‘Education for Life’, being the motto of RIMT World School, we impart holistic education in a congenial, invigorating environment, where the learners discover their talents, develop skills and grow up to be responsible citizens.
The school aims at :
Providing opportunities to each child to explore his capabilities & areas of interests.
Developing various facets of a child’s personality by providing quality education.
Preparing students to face the challenges of life so that they emerge victorious in all situations.
Sensitizing the learners towards their societal commitments.
