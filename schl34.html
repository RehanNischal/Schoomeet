They are trained, understand how children learn and grow, and recognize the unique qualities in each and every child. Curriculum is the perfect combination of several nationally recognized schools of thought, blended with our degreed teachers' own indigenous ideas. Our educators create the perfect learning experience for each child. We focus on how children learn and not just what they learn. All areas of early academic growth are addressed and skills are developed through layered, multi-sensory experiences over a child's infant, toddler, and preschool years. Play is at the center of our program, so activities and lessons are constructed for play with a purpose. Kids learn like school while having fun!
