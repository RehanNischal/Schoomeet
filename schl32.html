Tiny Tots Foundation School

The school promotes high standard of effective education for students at all stages of schooling through Pre-School and After school Learning concepts.

Mission is to Establish in and around south Chennai a minimum of three Institutions, to disseminate high standard of English and Mathematical Knowledge, aimed at about 150 students per annum by end 2020.

The school aims at creating an amiable environment through its unique infrastructure and activities for the overall personality development of the child.

The Method of Teacher-Led-Learning is shown a way out at Tiny Tots. Methodology include an innovative solution for the child's holistic development of Knowledge, Skill and Attitude (KSA).

Tiny Tots follow a system of Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) to augment reinforcement to the students on their areas requiring improvement.
