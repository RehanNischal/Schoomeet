Gurukul Global School

It has a high-tech infrastructure with 50 smart class rooms – Educomp modules, 3D Science Labs, Computer & Science Labs and Digital Library where teaching- learning process is student centred and ensures all round developments of students. It has a huge cricket field, 03 Basket ball courts, gym, tennis court and facilities for Handball, Football and Volleyball. It has a Cricket Academy in collaboration with PCA which is best in North India and coaches for other sports. It has NCC – Air Wing as well as Naval Wing. It has excelled in Cricket Basketball, Volleyball, Handball, Horse Riding, Swimming, and Taekwondo, Athletics, Aero – Modelling, Robotics, Fencing ; many literary, music, cultural, turban tying and divinity competitions at State and National Levels. Exchange programme, NASA workshop at U.S and many activities under Inter-act Club, Eco Club, and Rotary International are its annual features. Youth Parliament, Summer Camps, Behaviourial and Aptitude Tests, in Service Teacher Training Programmes and Seminars, Career Couselling and Educational Workshops are held regularly. Its Junior Wing is in Sector 37 and Senior Wing in Sector 36. Boys and Girls Hostel is in Senior Wing Campus and presently has 87 students.
