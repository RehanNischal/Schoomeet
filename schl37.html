Bright Future Preschool

The center is designed for children ages 1-5 years of age.

The program offers a hands on curriculum where children are encouraged to explore materials in order to stimulate cognitive, emotional, social, and physical growth.

Children will be engaged in many exciting and educational activities that promote learning.

It has a fully trained staff that is headed by two State of Florida Certified Teachers in Early Childhood.

It also offer Computer, Karate, gymnastics and Ballet Classes.

Breakfast, Lunch and Snack is offered for all full time students
