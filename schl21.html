Yadvindra Public School

Yadavindra Public School, Mohali, Punjab, near Chandigarh, is an institution based in Punjab. This school is a member of the Indian Public School Conference. This school is based on the lines of Public Schools. The School was established on 9 April 1979, under the patronage of Maharaja Amarinder Singh of Patiala, the son of Maharajdhiraj Yadavindra Singh of Patiala. The School is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examination (C.I.S.C.E.), New Delhi. Maj Gen TPS Waraich is the director of the school. Ms Nutan Budhiraja is Deputy Headmistress of the school. Yadavindra Public School, Mohali, was awarded the British Council International School Award for the period 2015-18. Yps changed its logo to the logo of YPS Patiala in February 2017 as they are the branches of the same school.
