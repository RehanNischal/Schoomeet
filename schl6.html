Divya Public School

Divya Public School (Affiliated to C.B.S.E.), co-educational founded by Shri Rattan Singh M.A., B.Ed., an ex-serviceman and a person devoted to cause of education.

It is run by Divya Educational Society Chandigarh (Regd.) with the aim of serving the human society in the field of education by helping the younger ones to grow into balanced personalities. the school is recognised by the Department of Education, U.T. Chandigarh Administration, Chandigarh.

It is said that the mother is the first teacher of a child. The role of school comes next is making him a responsible citizen. Though the role of the school is second to home in educating the child. It’s role is forming the personality of a child is most important. It is the responsibility of the teachers as well as the parents to make the child capable of facing all the challenges of life and coming out of victorious in the end.

The school has been achieving very good for past many years. A number of our student have been on merit list of Chandigarh administration. The results have never been less than 95 percent. The school curriculum has been divided in keeping in mind the studies, games, extra activities, annual functions and educational excursions, many important persons from different fields like educationists, social activists, religious leaders, great personalities in the political field have come and blessed the school and its students at different functions.
