Carmel Convent School

Started on July 16,1959, Carmel Convent Chandigarh has a spacious campus with well planned buildings and playgrounds. The school is recognized by the Directorate of Education of the UT administration. It is affiliated to the Central board of secondary education.

Objectives:
Our aim is to form integrated women:
Capable of responding to life’s challenges with joy and a deep faith in God.
Their choices and decisions are guided by right values.
Enriched by our cultural heritage.
Committed to the service of our people.
