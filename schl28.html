The British School

The British School, Panchkula under the patronage of Dr K.K. Khandelwal I.A.S, Additional Chief Secretary to the Chief Minister, Haryana, was founded by Shri T. R. Sethi and Mrs Urmila Sethi in January, 2005 The British School, Panchkula is run under the aegis of Urmila T R Sethi Global Foundation. We are guided by a steadfast belief in impassioned,enthusiastic participation- the idea of embracing every opportunity and cultivating the courage not only to try new things but to create new ways to learn and grow.

Shri T.R. Sethi said, “Our focus is learning. We want to enable students to become academically and personally successful people who are internationally minded and motivated to become lifelong learners and caring citizens prepared to take effective responsibility in an ever changing world”.
