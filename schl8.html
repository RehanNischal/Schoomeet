Shishu Niketan Model Senior Secandary School

Shishu Niketan Model Sr. Sec. School, Sector 22-D, Chandigarh came into existence in the year 1953 as the first private school of Chandigarh and the auspicious beginning so made, today proclaims its momentous accomplishments in academic as well as non- academic fields. Over the years we have grown into one of tri-city’s leading group of institutions, providing academic excellence to our students. This is facilitated by dedicated educators who are trained to channelize their energy and resources towards child-centered qualitative learning.

Affiliated to CBSE, the institution provides value based education and the teaching is done with a practical approach to ensure that the students have theoretical as well as practical knowledge of the subject. To accomplish this vision we have well equipped laboratories and smart classes.

The school offers the following streams for Senior Secondary classes - Medical, Non-Medical and Commerce and is dedicated towards fulfilling every child’s educational and developmental needs in a safe and caring environment conducive to learning and teaching. We are committed to provide a high quality academic curriculum along with varied co-curricular activities to ensure the overall development of child. We strive to equip the students morally, physically, intellectually and culturally to become worthy citizens of democratic India, to ardently guard and retain its noble traditions and make progress in a meticulous way.

At Shishu Niketan Model Sr. Sec. School, we strongly believe that the goal of education is to prepare children not only for earning their livelihood but also to empower them to shoulder their responsibilities. This is the spirit embedded in our school motto – “COME TO LEARN, GO TO SERVE”.
